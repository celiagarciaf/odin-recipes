Descripction

A salad is a dish, typically served cold, consisting of mixed ingredients, often vegetables, fruits, or meats, and usually dressed with a sauce or vinaigrette.

Salads can be as simple as a bowl of lettuce with dressing or more elaborate composed dishes with various ingredients arranged on a plate.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Oil
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Wash vegetables
  2. Cut vegetables
  3. Mix vegetables
  4. Add oil
  5. Add salt
  6. Mix
