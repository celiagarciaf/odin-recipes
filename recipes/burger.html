Descripction

A burger, or hamburger, is a sandwich consisting of a patty (usually ground beef) cooked and placed between two halves of a bun.

It's often served with various toppings and condiments, like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and sauces like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. Burgers are a popular and versatile food, with many international and regional variations.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cute vegetables
  2. Cook meat
  3. Toast bread
  4. Put vegetables and meat inside bread
  5. Add ketchup
  6. Close it with the other bread slice
